Spencer's PC Waranty:

At Spencer's PC, we understand that hardware issues can and will pop up. 

All PCs will be tested in-house before they become available to customers, and you may test out a PC before purchase if you wish.

 Any warranty will be voided if the damage is deemed to have been caused by the user.

See below for the specific warranty we have for products we sell. 

For New Computers:
By default, all new computers we sell will come with a 1 year warranty. This warranty only covers hardware issues caused by defective hardware. You may purchase up to a 5 year warranty from us, check in store for specific pricing.
For Used Computers:
All used computers come with a 6 month warranty.
For PC Parts:
All new PC parts will come with a 1 year warranty. Additional coverage is available, but due to limited supplies, we will often only offer a refund. We recommend you check to see if the manufacturer has a warranty extension option, as they will have your specific product in stock in their RMA produce if needed.
